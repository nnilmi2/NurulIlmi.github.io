Inspiration

I was inspired by both of her. Abigail Limuria with her lalita project and Intan Quro with her life experiences in Turkey. Maybe both of themm are not as cool as Kamala Harris or other world figures, but their persistance to process into extraordinary woman.

Abigail Limuria is CEO of Lalita Project. Lalita project is book that contains 51 inspiring Indonesian female figures.This book was written by two female students, Abigail Limuria and Grace Kadiman.

Intan Quro is Teacher in Istanbul,Turkey. She was chairman of Indonesia Student Association in Turkey (PPI) Ankara in the 2016/2017 period.Intan was also always active in activities held by the Indonessian embassy in Turkey.
